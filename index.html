Welcome to My Portfolio Website!

My name is Daniel (DJ) Granovetter. I grew up in Upstate New York and in Israel.

From a young age I was very much into math and logic puzzles. Math was always my best subject at school, and I read a lot of riddle and logic puzzles books. At age ten I taught myself to program on the Macintosh, using Hypertalk, and also learned animation and created my own simple games.

As an adult I've created platform games as a hobby using GML (Game Maker Language). In 2015 I learned to code in HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I have lots of patience and greatly enjoy working out all the logical factors of programming. I don't get bored or frustrated, I keep on going until I get the code just right.

Recently I've developed several mobile apps for Android using Adobe Phonegap.

I find it greatly satisfying to develop programs that solve problems and help people.
